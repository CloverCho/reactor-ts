Class RTIClient

An RTIClient is used within a federate to abstract the socket connection to the RTI and the RTI's binary protocol over the socket. RTIClient exposes functions for federate-level operations like establishing a connection to the RTI or sending a message. RTIClient is an EventEmitter, and asynchronously emits events for: 'startTime', 'connected', 'message', 'timedMessage', and 'timeAdvanceGrant'. The federatedApp is responsible for handling the events to ensure a correct exeuction.
